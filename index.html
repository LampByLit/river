Reflections represents the unique combination of premium hospitality with plant medicine therapy in the beating heart of the jungle, Iquitos, the Capital of the Amazon. Accessible only by air or river, Reflections offers a unique opportunity for spiritual healing, self-discovery, and deep connection with nature among the culture of the ancient Peruvian rainforest.

The Maloka

Built directly on the river, the Maloka provides ceremonies led by experienced, renowned Maestros, healers and locals of the serene Amazonian wild, honoring ancient wisdom with modern comfort. Guided eco-tours explore the surrounding wilderness for its flora and fauna.

The Restaurant and Pool

Enjoy gourmet meals and experience the Amazonian jungle from the comfort of the relaxing eatery and cafe, including live performatnces, or sumberge yourself in the refreshing onsite swimming pool, available for guests to use and locals alike.

The Conference Center

Conduct meetings, workshops, or events in the spacious hall, host to local festivals, musical performances, and other cultural occasions. Explore the adventures of the Amazon from the distinction of Iquitos' hidden gem,
Reflections River Retreat.
